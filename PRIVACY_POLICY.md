# ByteDump Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 28, 2024**

## Overview

ByteDump is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how ByteDump handles your data.

## Data Collection

**ByteDump does NOT collect, transmit, or store any user data.**

Specifically:
- ❌ No analytics or tracking
- ❌ No crash reporting
- ❌ No usage statistics
- ❌ No personal information collection
- ❌ No internet connectivity
- ❌ No third-party services or SDKs

## How ByteDump Works

ByteDump is a **completely offline** file encryption tool that:

✅ Only accesses files you explicitly select through file dialogs
✅ Performs all encryption/decryption locally on your device
✅ Never transmits any data over the network
✅ Never stores data outside of the files you choose to save

## Permissions

ByteDump only requests permission to:
- **Read and write user-selected files**: Required to encrypt and decrypt files you choose

The app is sandboxed and cannot access any files outside of those you explicitly select.

## Your Data Security

All encryption happens locally on your Mac:
- Files are encrypted using AES-256-GCM
- Passwords are never stored or transmitted
- Encrypted files remain on your device
- No cloud storage or remote servers are used

## Third-Party Services

ByteDump does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

## Children's Privacy

ByteDump does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
